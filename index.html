About Us

We are a community of self-learners passionate about building, collaborating, talking about and teaching software development. We organize free and open meetups every weekend on a variety of topics, find out more in the meetups section.

Meetups

We organize free and open meetups every weekend. Most of them are Pair Programming Meetups, where we come together to pair up and learn or build something. Sometimes we organize sessions to teach a topic, and sometimes we organize fun events like coding competitions and hackathons. Follow us on Facebook and join our Meetup group to receive updates for future meetups.
